As the agent of record, I am requesting the following corrections and amendments to the above referenced policy. The policy was issued using the backdated illustration (Joyce Mineart nearest age 71). These changes are to be of the same effect as if contained in the application.

Requested Amendments — Stephen Mineart

  • The policy was issued with the Estate Preservation Rider
  • The Face Amount excluding riders is $6,000,000.00
  • The primary beneficiaries of this policy should read: Sarah Mineart, Child, 34%; Julie Mineart, Child, 33% and Mark Mineart, Child, 33%
  • The policy is issued without secondary beneficiary.
  • The policy was issued with the Healthy Engagement Rider
  • The details to present alcohol consumption should read: I have not consumed alcohol in the past 10 years
  • The answer to “What is the total amount of new Life Insurance coverage that you plan to accept with all companies including this application?” should read: $6,000,000
  • The Planned Premium should read: $120,000.00 annually
  • The policy should be issued using the current age illustration (Joyce Mineart age 70, Preferred Non-Smoker) per the approved offer dated 03/12/2026
Reference

The approved offer (03/12/2026) included two illustrations: (1) backdated to 12/15/2025 and (2) current age. The current age illustration reflects Joyce Mineart at age 70 with a significantly higher face amount at the same underwriting approval. The client has reviewed both and selected the current age option at a reduced premium of $120,000.
