"With the inheritance bridging Year 1, your Nassau benefit base gets one more year of growth. Starting Year 2, the GMIB covers the premium AND most of the taxes. Your out-of-pocket is $5,000 to $7,000 a year — and your children receive $6 million, tax-free. There's no investment that competes with that math."

5 minutes
→
Ask directly: "Based on what we've reviewed, does Option B feel right for your situation?"
→
If yes → proceed to paperwork. If questions → address them. If they want Option A or C → adjust accordingly.
→
Present the Client Decision Confirmation. Walk through the four checkboxes. Get signatures from both Stephen and Joyce.

The form only has one Owner signature line — have Stephen sign it, then Joyce signs directly below or next to it. Archer signs as Agent. All three signatures on one form.

"When we submitted your application, we went in at the maximum premium to make sure there were no issues with approval. Now that you're approved, we're adjusting to the exact option you've selected — $120,000 premium, $6 million death benefit. These forms tell John Hancock to make that adjustment."
