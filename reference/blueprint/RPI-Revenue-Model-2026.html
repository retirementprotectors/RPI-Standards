Section 01

How The Machine Creates Revenue

Three integrated platforms. Each one serves a different channel. Together they compound — every client acquired through one channel creates revenue opportunities across all three.

B2C — Direct Client Revenue

PRODASHX

The daily workspace for Sales and Service. Where client relationships become revenue.

  • CLIENT360 shows every gap — client has annuity but no life? That's a conversation waiting to happen.
  • Pipeline Stages (Orange to Green) ensure no lead falls through the cracks. 80% close rate.
  • QUE-Medicare quotes, compares, and generates recommendation letters in one meeting. 4x AEP throughput.
  • RMD Center turns compliance deadlines into proactive service calls and repositioning conversations.
  • QuickIntake AI onboards accounts in minutes, not days. Faster onboarding = faster first transaction.
B2B — Growth Multiplier

SENTINEL (DAVID)

Revenue scales in chunks, not one client at a time. Acquisitions and partnerships.

  • M&A Pipeline manages acquisition targets from identification through integration.
  • Agent Onboarding brings producing agents onto RPI's platform — generating override commissions.
  • Book Integration absorbs acquired client databases into The Machine in hours, not months.
  • Gradient / Schwab / RBC channels drive AUM growth — compounding advisory fees.
B2E — Capacity Multiplier

RIIMO

Doesn't generate revenue directly — multiplies the revenue capacity of every person on the team. Every hour saved on admin is an hour in front of a client.

  • CAM catches commission discrepancies. Zero revenue leakage across hundreds of accounts.
  • C3 Campaigns (60 campaigns, 661 templates) generate warm leads automatically.
  • DEX automates document processing. 5+ hours/week per agent back to selling.
  • Command Center gives leadership real-time pipeline visibility for faster decisions.

The Acquisition-to-Revenue Flywheel

STEP 1
SENTINEL acquires a 1,000-client book (Medicare, Life, or Mixed)
STEP 2
The Machine ingests all client data — CLIENT360 immediately shows product gaps
STEP 3
C3 fires targeted campaigns. Pipeline fills. Team works Orange to Green.
STEP 4
Cross-sell: Medicare clients get Life, Annuity, and Advisory (AUM)
STEP 5
Renewals compound every year. AUM fees compound. Recurring stacks.
STEP 6
Revenue from Step 5 funds the next acquisition. Cycle repeats — faster.

The Three Revenue Multipliers

1. Systematic Cross-Sell

CLIENT360 shows every product gap across 18,000+ clients. C3 campaigns fire automatically. The team doesn't have to remember who needs what — the system tells them.

Example: Midwest Medigap's 6,000 Medicare-only clients. Even a 1.5% annuity conversion = $13.5M in new premium = $1.27M in gross revenue.

2. Acquisition Velocity

Most agencies take 3–6 months to integrate a book. The Machine does it in days. That means RPI can acquire more books per year, realize revenue faster, and cross-sell sooner.

At 3+ acquisitions per year averaging 1,000 clients each, the client base grows 15–20% annually from M&A alone.

3. Recurring Revenue Compounding

Every MAPD enrollment, every AUM dollar, every policy renewal adds to the recurring base. By Year 3, $3.5M in recurring revenue arrives before a single new sale. That base funds operations, funds acquisitions, and drives enterprise value. A $14.8M agency with 24% recurring and growing AUM is worth materially more than its revenue suggests.
