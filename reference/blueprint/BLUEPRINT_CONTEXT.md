## DAVID Thesis

**D**isruptive **A**nd **V**ertically **I**ntegrated **D**istribution

Core insight: It doesn't matter where the clients come from. Partnerships, mergers, acquisitions -- every client hits the same Machine the same way. That's the disruption.

The sequence:
1. **Handle the smoke** (RIIMO) -- build capacity first
2. **Scale DAVID** (SENTINEL) -- once capacity exists, open the floodgates
3. **Compound** (PRODASHX) -- Medicare entry (cheap multiples) -> cross-sell to Life/Annuity -> manufacture AUM (expensive multiples)
4. **ESOP** -- the team that built it owns 30% by 2031 ($4.8B employee equity)

## Valuation Multiple Framework

| Stage | Growth Multiple | Earnings Multiple | Applied To |
|-------|----------------|-------------------|-----------|

| Scale | G-4x-5x | E-8x-10x | $36M EBITDA = $288-360M |

Key multiple drivers:
- Recurring revenue % (MAPD renewals + AUM fees) increases valuation multiples
- MAPD books: 1-1.5x multiple
- AUM books: 6-8x multiple on fees
- Tech-enabled platform premium vs. traditional agency valuation
- National footprint + scalable infrastructure
