How The Machine Creates Revenue

Three integrated platforms. The B2C engine generates revenue per client. The B2B engine acquires clients at scale. The B2E engine multiplies capacity to handle the volume. Together: a national roll-up machine.

B2C — Revenue Per Client

PRODASHX

  • CLIENT360 shows every product gap — cross-sell from Medicare into Life, Annuity, and Advisory.
  • Pipeline Stages (Orange to Green) ensure no lead falls through the cracks. 80% close rate.
  • QUE-Medicare quotes + compares + generates rec letters in one meeting. 4x AEP throughput.
  • RMD Center turns compliance deadlines into repositioning conversations.
  • QuickIntake AI onboards accounts in minutes, not days.
B2B — Clients At Scale

SENTINEL / DAVID

  • M&A Pipeline manages 24 acquisitions/year (2026) scaling to 144/year (2027).
  • Book Integration absorbs agency databases into The Machine in days, not months.
  • Agent Onboarding brings producing agents onto RPI — override commissions at scale.
  • National Distribution partnerships multiply reach without acquisition cost.
B2E — Capacity To Handle The Smoke

RIIMO

The Machine doesn't break when you go from 6,000 to 90,000 clients. Every process is systematized. Every integration follows the same playbook.

  • CAM tracks commissions across 181 agencies. Zero leakage.
  • C3 Campaigns fire automatically for every acquired client base.
  • DEX automates document processing at any volume.
  • Command Center gives leadership visibility across the national footprint.
